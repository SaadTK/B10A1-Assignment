Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of developing new ideas or solutions that significantly improve existing systems or solve complex problems.

Sustainability

Sustainability involves developing systems that meet the needs of the present while preserving resources for future generations.

Infrastructure

It encompasses the physical and organizational structures necessary for societal development, roads, energy, and communication systems.

Empowerment

Empowerment is about providing individuals and communities with the tools and resources they need to improve their own lives.
